Warriors Of Light

Battle of Twilight Town

The Story so far: Being obsessed and corrupted by the power of darkness. Xehanort is marching his army of death to every corners of every worlds. You have been assigned to protect Twilight Town. Protect it, even at the cost of your life.

Instruction: Select your Hero of Light and banish the minions of darkness.

Hint: - If this is your first time playing the game, pick Sora! Don't forget to use magic!

This game is base on Kingdom Hearts title from Square Enix.
